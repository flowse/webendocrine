Every gemstone is unique - and tells a story…

In our laboratories, we closely examine your gemstone and unearth that story with our world-class expertise. Every facet, inclusion and sparkling surface is carefully catalogued and painstakingly verified. It’s beauty may be subjective, but it’s value can be measured by a specific set of characteristics.

If you’re a jewelry retailer, you know your customers are keenly seeking quality and want to be reassured that what they are purchasing is the real thing. When you purchase a house you receive a deed. A vehicle comes with a title and registration. It only makes sense to document something as precious as a gem purchase with an official accreditation.

Our mission is to create in-depth reports with detailed descriptions of your diamonds, colored stones and jewelry. These reports are a mark of assurance for any buyer who wants to purchase diamonds confidently.

Services

Diamond Report

A diamond grading report describes the characteristics of your diamond. Of course, no two diamonds are exactly alike. Even when diamonds are similar in size, they may have widely different values depending on their other qualities.

Gemstone Identification Report

New technology makes it easier than ever to artificially color gemstones. That’s why it’s essential to have a scientific report that ensures the veracity of your colored gemstones. We will carefully examine every gemstone using our state-of-the-art technology, so you can attest to its quality.

Jewelry Report

The report must take into account all aspects of the finished piece of jewelry, from the materials used to the specifics of the mounting to the details of the craftsmanship. ٠

Diamond Sealing

When conducting reports, we seal the diamonds for security and protection purposes. We enclose a summary of the diamond’s report. Once the seal is broken a chemically generated pattern is released.
