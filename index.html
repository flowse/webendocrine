Objectives



To enhance knowledge and improve practice standards, especially among members of the society and specialists in the field.


To create opportunities for development and progress for members and specialists in the field.


To promote and lead a culture of sharing and exchange of ideas and experience among institutions and societies within and outside the kingdom.


To provide professional opinions/statements to health authorities.


To conduct relevant studies and provide specific professional advices to promote better levels for care in certain areas of interest.


To set standards of practice and monitor performance in various.


To promote health awareness among the public.

About the society

The Saudi Society of Endocrinology and Metabolism (SSEM) is a scientific health society in the field of Endocrinology, Diabetes and Metabolism for both adults and pediatrics with its services covering all provinces over the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. It was established under the umbrella of the Saudi Council for Health Specialties (SCFHS) on 29th. August 2009.
